# PinPost — Getting Started

## What is PinPost?

PinPost lets your team check in at every job site with one tap. Each check-in automatically posts to your Google Business Profile, helping you rank higher on Google Maps. More check-ins = more visibility = more customers finding you.

## How to access the app

1. Open: **https://pinpost-web-production.up.railway.app**
2. Log in with the credentials I gave you
3. You'll see your Dashboard

## How it works

- **You** manage the app from the Dashboard (add workers, view check-ins, connect Google)
- **Your workers** open the app on their phone, fill in the job address, snap a photo, and hit submit
- The app writes a professional post using AI and publishes it to Google Maps automatically

## Setting up

### Step 1: Connect your Google Business Profile
Go to **Settings** → click **Connect Google** → sign in with the Google account that owns your business profile → select your business location.

### Step 2: Add your workers
Go to **Team** → click **Add Worker** → enter their name, email, and a password → share the login with them.

### Step 3: Workers install the app
Workers open the link on their phone in Chrome → tap the browser menu → **"Add to Home Screen"**. Now it works like a regular app.
